Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device.

Calm New Tab is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect — which is essentially nothing.

Data We Collect

None.

  • We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.
  • We do not collect browsing history, search queries, or any user behavior data.

Permissions

  • Identity (Google Login): Used solely for connecting to your Google Photos account to display your personal photos as backgrounds. This is an optional feature. Authentication tokens are handled locally by your browser and are never sent to our servers.
  • Geolocation: Used solely to fetch local weather data from the free Open-Meteo API. Your coordinates are sent directly to Open-Meteo and are not stored or logged anywhere.

Third-Party Services

  • Pexels API — Used to fetch nature video backgrounds. No personal data is sent to Pexels.
  • Open-Meteo API — Used to fetch weather data based on your approximate location. No personal data is sent beyond coordinates.
  • Google Photos API — Optional. Used only if you choose to connect your Google Photos. Authentication is handled by Chrome's built-in identity system.

Data Storage

Settings are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.sync. No data leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we will update it here.
